Caliz Skate Shop

ABOUT

Our space serves as a venue for worry-free jamming. We think of it as a musical haven for musicians and bands, and a place for singers and songwriters to enjoy music.

It has just started last 2019 of September, but was planned way back 6 years ago. A place built for artists to let music flow in their hearts and enrich their soul.

THE FOUNDER

IAN ANDANAR

An entrepreneur who is passionate about music and the creative world. He is the founder and chief executive officer of Caliz Music Studio, a speakeasy-themed music studio venue in Angeles City for music creators and lovers alike. Ian Andanar is the guitarist of the band D2NATION in early 00’s until 2009.
