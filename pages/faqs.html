Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

We are located Stall-9, 3rd Floor, 5 G Building, Nepomuceno Street, Barangay Santo Rosario, Angeles City, Pampanga.

How much does it cost per session?

It cost 250 pesos per hour.

How to reserve or book a session?

You may send us an email or message us on Facebook. Be sure to reserve your session 3 hours prior to your chosen hour.

Do you offer sound recording and production?

No, we do not offer sound recording and production. It is only a jamming space for artists and bands.

Do you sell equipments?

No, we don't sell equipments.

What is Caliz Skate Shop?

Caliz Skate Shop is a companion of the music studio. Located at the same bulding of the studio, it is a place mainly for skating enthusiasts.
