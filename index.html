ABOUT US

Caliz Music Studio, located at Angeles City in Pampanga, serves as a space for worry-free jamming. We think of it as a musical santuary for musicians and bands, and a place for singers and songwriters to enjoy music. It has just started last 2019 of September, but was planned way back 6 years ago. A haven built for artists to let music flow in their hearts and enrich their soul.
